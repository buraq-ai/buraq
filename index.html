Opposition Tactics

What We Counter

How abusers try to evade accountability — and how Buraq AI is built to neutralize those tactics.

Camera Tampering & Blackouts

Cut feeds, smash lenses, or kill power. Redundant sensing, local storage, and attested timestamps preserve evidence even during outages.

RF Jamming & GPS Spoofing

Adversaries jam signals or spoof position. Edge autonomy and multi-sensor geotagging maintain trusted location context.

Thermal Masking

Smoke, darkness, and thermal decoys. Multi-band detection and temporal tracking expose patterns attempts to hide.

Decoys & Denial

Throwaway sites and disguised assets. Cross-view correlation reduces false narratives and highlights material links.

Metadata Stripping

Images re-shared without EXIF to erase origin. Edge-side signatures, hashes, and logs preserve verifiable provenance.

Coordinated Disinformation

False reports flood channels to bury truth. Consensus signals and narrative-graphing surface credible, corroborated events.
